Hello, welcome to the Manga section! For those who do may not know, Manga is a style of Japanese comic books and graphic novels. Manga is purely hand-drawn and has some very cool sketeches. Manga is also mainly focused on Japanese comic books.

One Punch Man

One Punch man is a Japanese superhero manga that follows the story of Saitama, a superhero who, because he can defeat any apponent with a single punch, grows bored from a lack of a challenge.

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series that follows Monkey D. Luffy and his quest to become the pirate king!

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is a Japanese manga series that follows Eren Jaegar on his quest to save his people and to learn more about the world outside of the walls.

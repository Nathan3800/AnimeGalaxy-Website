Hello, welcome to the Manhua section! For those who do may not know, Manhua is Chinese manga. Manhua is purely hand-drawn and has some very cool sketeches. Manhua is also mainly focused on cultivation.

The Urban Immortal Cultivator

The Urban Immortal Cultivator is focused on a character named Chen Fan who was once a great cultivator but died and reincarnated due to failing to transcend the Tribulation of Heaven.

The Beginning After the End

The Beginning After the End follows a King Grey who despite his unmatched strength and success, his life lacks fulfillment. After he mysteriously died, he was reborn as Arthur Leywin in a magical world which offered him a chance for a new and meaningful beginning.

I am The Sorcerer King

Sorcerer King is follows Lee SungHoon who lives in a world where people have started to awaken powers and began hunting monsters. Lee SungHoon was born without any powers until an accident occured where he remembered his past as the overpowered Sorcerer King Kratraus.
